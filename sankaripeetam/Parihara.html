Graha Shanti Pooja

Graha Shanti Pooja is a powerful ritual performed to pacify the nine planetary energies influencing your life. By offering to specific mantras, sacred materials, and homa flames to each graha, this pooja brings balance, removes obstacles, and attracts harmony, health, and prosperity to Graha Shanti Pooja.

Kaalasarpa Dosha Shanthi Pooja

Kaalasarpa Dosha Shanti Pooja remedies the malefic effects that arise when all planets fall between Rahu and Ketu in your chart. Through precise rituals, mantra chanting, and holy offerings, health issues, and financial setbacks—restoring peace and stability.

Mruthyunjaya Homam

Mrityunjaya Homam is a sacred fire ceremony dedicated to Lord Shiva as the conqueror of death. Chanting the Maha Mrityunjaya Mantra while offering ghee, herbs, and ingredients into the homa fire brings healing, longevity, and protection from accidents and fears. It revitalizes body, mind, and spirit under divine grace.

Chandi Homam

Chandi Homam is a powerful fire ritual dedicated to Goddess Durga. It is performed to remove obstacles, destroy negative forces, and bring protection, strength, and success.

Dasha Mahaa Vidyaa Homam

This homam is dedicated to the ten powerful forms of the Divine Mother (Dasha Mahavidyas). It grants spiritual awakening, power, and liberation from fear and ignorance.

Ashta Lakshmi Homam

Ashta Lakshmi Homam is conducted to invoke the eight forms of Goddess Lakshmi, each representing a unique form of wealth—spiritual, material, knowledge, courage,

Aarogyavrudhi Homam

Performed for enhanced health and vitality, Aarogyavrudhi Homam helps to remove ailments, strengthen immunity, and ensure physical and mental well-being.

Maha Soura Paashupatha Homam

Dedicated to Lord Surya and Lord Shiva, this rare and powerful homam is performed to attain spiritual elevation, strength, and ultimate liberation from karmic bondage.

Ayushvrudhi Homam

This homam is performed for longevity and overall life prosperity. It is ideal for those seeking good health and a long, purposeful life blessed by divine energy.

Aruna Paarayanam

Aruna Paarayanam involves the sacred chanting of hymns dedicated to the Sun God. Practiced during Surya Namaskara, it enhances personal aura, vitality, and attracts prosperity and positive energy.

Pratyeka Pooja

Pratyeka Pooja is a personalized ritual tailored to individual needs and concerns. Guided by the advice of spiritual gurus or astrologers, it is performed to seek divine intervention for health, peace, or success.
